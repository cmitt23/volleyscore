## Quick help
- Make sure your iPhone and Apple Watch are paired.
- If the watch app isn’t updating, open the iPhone app once to re-establish connection.
- Try force-quitting the watch app and reopening.

## Privacy
VolleyScore Watch does not collect, store, or sell personal data.
